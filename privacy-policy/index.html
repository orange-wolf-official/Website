INTRO

Orange Wolf Dijital Oyun Teknolojileri Anonim Şirketi (“Orange Wolf” or “we”, “us”, “our”) is a technology company that designs and develops mobile games. With this Privacy Policy, we aim to explain how and why we gather, store, share, and use your personal data in a manner consistent with the laws of the countries where we do business. When this Policy refers to “personal data” in this Policy, we mean any information that can be used to identify, relate to, characterize, or reasonably be linked, either directly or indirectly, with a specific natural person or household, as well as any other information that falls under the definition of “personal data” or “personally identifiable information.” This privacy policy covers all of the following: websites, mobile applications, blogs, forums, offline services, our advertising and promotional activities across all platforms, and any other services we might occasionally offer to you (collectively, the “Services”). Employees, job candidates, and other personnel of Orange Wolf are not covered by this privacy policy. Orange Wolf oversees the management of the information we possess pertaining to you in relation to your utilization of the Services. In essence, we decide and bear the responsibility for the utilization of your personal data. According to this Privacy Policy, your personal data will be handled to facilitate the provision of the Services following your download of our mobile applications, engagement with our games, or interaction with our Services. Should you have any reservations about furnishing us with data or its utilization as outlined in this Privacy Policy, you can reach out to us and exercise the rights detailed in the Security section of this Privacy Policy.

HOW WE COLLECT YOUR PERSONAL DATA

We might gather personal information either directly from you or through third-party entities with whom you have granted consent or who possess legal authorization to share such data with us (including advertising platforms, partners, and data aggregators). This information may encompass details about your attributes, interests, other games and services you engage with, as well as demographic and general location information. The utilization of your personal data will align with the provisions outlined in this Privacy Policy.

Personal Data Collected Directly from You

Orange Wolf processes your personal data with the purpose of delivering the Services when you download, access, and/or engage with our games or utilize our other Services. The majority of this data is obtained directly from you, and we collect the following information in connection with our Services:

  1. Contact Information: This includes details like your country or region and username.
  2. User Generated Content: Encompassing files, documents, videos, images, data, or information you opt to upload or transmit through your interactions with us or your utilization of the Services.
  3. Profile and Gameplay Information: This comprises your screen name, avatar or profile picture; in-game scores, achievements, and purchases; your participation in competitions, as well as your membership and involvement in teams on the Services.
  4. Survey Information: This involves the feedback and ratings you provide regarding our Services. Inferences: Information derived from your usage of the Services, presenting predictions about your interests and preferences.
  5. Parental Information: If you activate parental control settings in the Services, this may include the parent’s email address and PIN.
  6. We utilize this data to furnish our games and Services to you, which includes optimizing our Services, tailoring in-game offers, and validating purchases. Additionally, we may employ it for other purposes outlined in this Privacy Policy.

Information We Collect Automatically

Depending on your interaction with specific Services we offer, we may collect and process various types of information, including: Analytics Data: This involves details about your usage and interactions with our games, advertising, and other Services. Examples include information on when and how you use our Services, the device you use for access, and technical diagnostics such as battery life, loading time, latency, framerate, or crash logs pertaining to the performance of our Services on your device. Device Data: This includes your unique device ID (persistent/non-persistent), Identifier For Advertisers (IDFA), Identifier for Vendor (IDFV), and game center ID. Location Data: This encompasses your location based on your Internet Protocol (“IP”). Commercial Information: Such as details about products and services purchased, obtained, or considered, as well as other histories or tendencies related to purchasing or consumption. We and our third-party providers may utilize technologies like cookies (small data files stored on an individual’s computer), web beacons, pixels, embedded scripts, location-identifying technologies, and logging technologies (collectively referred to as “cookies”) to deliver our website services. For more detailed information, please refer to the Cookies section of this Privacy Policy.

Personal Data Collected from Third Parties

As previously stated, the majority of the data we get about you is provided by you when you use our services or play our games. On the other hand, we also obtain information from third parties that you authorize to access your personal data (for example, by installing our mobile applications through the App Store or Google Store and connecting your Facebook account to mobile applications).

  • Social Media: We may receive information about individuals that they allow the social network to share with third parties when you interact with our Services through different social media networks. Examples of these interactions include when someone “Likes” us on Facebook, follows us, or shares our content on Google, Facebook, Twitter, or other social networks. Depending on a user’s privacy choices on the social network, we may obtain information about them that includes their name, profile image, and friend list. Before sharing information or connecting to other services, users should always check and, if needed, modify their privacy settings on third-party websites, social media networks, and services. We use this information for the purpose of running, maintaining, and offering you the features and functionalities of the Services. We also use it for direct communication, such as sending you emails about goods and services that could be of interest to you.
  • Service Providers: Orange Wolf does not have access to your credit card or bank account information when you install or purchase things related to our mobile apps (such as those available through the App Store or Google Play). However, the previously mentioned third-party payment processors may share non-financial information about your purchases with us, such as the items purchased to fulfill your order. Personal data obtained from third-party firms will be subject to the privacy settings of the relevant third-party applications.
  • Authentication Services: Certain aspects of our Services offer you the option to log in using a third-party social network or authentication service, such as Facebook. These services serve to authenticate your identity and provide you with the opportunity to share specific personal data with us. This may include details like your name, email address, user ID associated with the third-party, profile picture, and the names and avatars of your Facebook friends who are already engaged with our games. The extent of data we receive is contingent upon the policies of the third party and your privacy settings on that particular third-party site. Where applicable, you’ll also have the ability to view which of your friends are participating in the same game, monitor their progress, and allow them to see your advancements. Additionally, various functionalities within our games and other Services, such as inviting friends to play, may be accessible. It’s important to note that, during the initial association of your Facebook account with your Orange Wolf profile, you have the choice to withhold access to your email address through the Facebook dialog box. Logging in with Facebook does not grant us permission to post anything to your page without your explicit consent, and we do not receive information about your Facebook friends who do not engage with our games. If you prefer not to have your Facebook friends view your progress within our games, you can prevent this by disconnecting from Facebook within our games and adjusting your app permissions from within your Facebook settings. Opting not to create an Orange Wolf profile by linking your Facebook account means that the personal data we collect during your gameplay will be associated with a random identifier rather than direct identifying information. However, we will still collect device-level identifiers and other information associated with those identifiers, as previously described. We recommend that you read Facebook’s Privacy Policy and review your privacy settings from time to time to ensure that you understand fully how your Facebook data is shared.
  • Other Sources: We may also collect personal data about individuals that we do not otherwise have from, for example, publicly available sources, third-party data providers, brand partnerships, or through transactions such as mergers and acquisitions. We use this information to operate, maintain, and provide you with the features and functionality of our Services.
  • Other Sources: Additionally, we may acquire personal data about individuals from sources other than direct interactions, such as publicly available sources, third-party data providers, brand partnerships, or through transactions like mergers and acquisitions. This information is employed to ensure the operation, maintenance, and provision of features and functionality within our Services.

We engage in interest-based advertising and collaborate with third-party advertising companies to deliver targeted advertisements based on your browsing history. We authorize third-party online advertising networks, social media companies, and other third-party services to gather information about your usage of our online services over time. This data is used to display ads on our Services, other websites or services you may use, and across various devices you utilize. Our advertising partners may enhance ad targeting by combining your personal data with information they have collected about you from other sources. They gather this data when you use their services or visit third-party websites. Our partners use this information to make assumptions about your preferences and serve you advertisements tailored to your interests, including ads for Orange Wolf’ Services when you use other websites and services. To facilitate these processes, we utilize ad identifiers and similar technologies, allowing our third-party advertising partners and us to:

  • Recognize you and your device.
  • Provide interest-based advertising.
  • Enable interactions between our Services and third-party social networks or platforms, if you have granted permission for such interaction.
  • Enable the provision of more customized services, such as delivering our Services in the correct language.

The information used for interest-based advertising is typically collected through tracking technologies, including cookies, web beacons, embedded scripts, location-identifying technologies, and similar tools. These technologies identify the device you are using and collect various details, such as click stream information, browser type, visit time and date, AdID, precise location data, and more. We may share a common account identifier, like a hashed email address or user ID, with our third-party advertising partners to recognize you across different devices. This information is utilized by our third-party partners and us to enhance the relevance of online advertisements, providing advertising-related services such as reporting, attribution, analytics, and market research. Additionally, we may use services from third parties, like social media platforms, to deliver targeted ads. This may involve providing a hashed version of your email address or other information to the platform provider. Our website currently employs strictly necessary, functional, analytical/performance, and advertising/targeting cookies. Please refer to the Cookies section of this Privacy Policy for more detailed information.

COOKIES

Our website utilizes the following types of cookies, similar to most online services:

  1. Strictly Necessary Cookies: Essential for the proper functioning of our website.
  2. Functional Cookies: Enable the website to remember information that alters its behavior or appearance, such as preferred language or region. The legal basis for using functionality cookies is our legitimate interests in maintaining our website.
  3. Analytical/Performance Cookies: Allow recognition and counting of visitors, aiding in the improvement of website functionality and understanding visitor movement.
  4. Advertising/Targeting Cookies: Operate during your visit to our website, particularly in the presence of videos linked to third-party streaming platforms. They track visitors’ online activity on our website to display more relevant advertisements.

It’s important to note that when playing our games, unless you opt-out of interest-based advertising (as explained in this section), your personal data may be used by our marketing partners to collect and utilize information about you. This is done to enhance advertising, targeting, and measurement systems, as described in their respective privacy policies. You have the option to configure your web browser to notify you about attempts to place cookies on your computer or restrict the types of cookies allowed. However, please be aware that cookies used by our website operate differently than browser cookies, and standard cookie management tools in web browsers may not effectively remove them. Disabling cookies may result in the loss of certain features and functionality on our website, as cookies are essential for tracking and enhancing user activities. To make opt-out requests related to mobile apps on your device for businesses participating in the DAA’s CCPA App-based Opt-Out Tool, you can download the appropriate app at https://www.privacyrights.info/.

CONTROL OVER YOUR INFORMATION

You have several options to control the personal data collected by our Services:

  1. Delete or Reset Services:
    • Delete our games from your mobile device.
    • Reset your mobile advertising identifier.
  2. Control Device Information Access:
    • Manage the Services’ access to your device information through your device’s “Settings” app.

  3. Modify or Delete Information:
    • Contact us using the methods described in the Contact Us section for assistance with reviewing, modifying, or deleting your information. Note that modification or deletion may not be possible in all circumstances.
  4. Push Notifications:
    • Opt-out of push notifications for game updates, high scores, and other notices by adjusting the settings on your mobile device.

  7. Opting out of Facebook Advertising:
    • Update advertising preferences on Facebook by logging into your account and adjusting settings. Disconnect your Facebook or other social network account from our games if desired.
  8. Cookies:
    • Refer to the Cookies section of this Privacy Policy for detailed information on controlling your cookie settings.

These options provide you with control over the personal data collected and used by our Services, allowing you to tailor your experience to your preferences.
